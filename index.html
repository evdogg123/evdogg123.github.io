Day Tripper

Developed a road trip planner that is tightly integrated with the Google Maps API. Users can create trips, add stops, plan their route, and allocate days for each location. Restaurants or points of interest can be pinned for each of their stops. Additionally users can rearrange the order that the stops are visited and updated drive times and mileage are displayed.

Used stack:

  • html
  • css
  • NodeJS
  • Angular
  • MongoDB
